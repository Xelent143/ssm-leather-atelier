# MOTOGRIP GEAR — Page One Men's Jackets Visual System

Mode: built-in image generation and reference-led image editing.

The competitor collection was used only to inventory factual product names,
colors, prices, sale prices, sizes, and broad style categories. All MOTOGRIP
copy and imagery are original.

## Locked visual direction

- Warm ivory seamless studio backgrounds for product views.
- Warm limestone architectural setting for model views.
- Restrained, premium, contemporary leather atelier styling.
- Realistic leather grain, metal hardware, accurate garment construction.
- 4:5 vertical ecommerce composition unless a close-up is square.
- No competitor branding, copied artwork, watermarks, or supplier marks.

## Per-product prompt set

1. Product front: ghost-mannequin front view, full garment visible.
2. Product back: edit from the approved front, preserving construction.
3. Product side: edit from the approved front, preserving construction.
4. Close-up: leather, seam, collar, pocket, and hardware detail.
5. Infographic: warm ivory, charcoal, and copper feature callouts.
6. Model front: adult male model, charcoal knit, black tailored trousers.
7. Lifestyle: adult male model, refined motorcycle-heritage location.

## Completed reference batch

`Dean Brown Leather Biker Jacket` is the visual-quality reference for the
remaining page-one production batches. Its seven assets were generated from
one approved master product front to keep color, shoulder construction,
hardware, and proportions consistent.

The infographic uses the approved project asset
`assets/motogrip-logo-transparent-v2.png` as its brand reference. Generated
or substituted wordmarks are not approved for final infographic production.
